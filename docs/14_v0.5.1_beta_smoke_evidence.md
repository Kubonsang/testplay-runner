## Notes

- All verification is unit-test-based with injected fake runners. Exit 9 is
  tested by injecting a failing `ResultStore` or making artifact directories
  read-only during the fake runner's execution.
- The `TestService_ArtifactWriteFailure_ReturnsExit9` test uses `os.Chmod`
  to make the run directory read-only while the fake runner executes, causing
  `SaveSummary`/`SaveManifest` to fail without affecting `Store.Save`.
- Streaming log write failures (`stdout.log`, `stderr.log`) during Unity
  execution are not covered by exit 9 — this is a known limitation documented
  in the release notes and deferred to v0.6+.
